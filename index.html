Hello, I'am Arun Mandot

A clairvoyant’s business tycoon born on November 21 st , comes from a humble background, Son of Late Shri Rang Lal Mandot and Smt. Tipu Bai Mandot, nurtures a sense of pride in roots that lie in the great tradition and glorious history of soil of Mewar, Having lost his father at a teenage of 7, the burden of nurturing the family came on the shoulder of elder brothers. At the age of 11, Arun shared the responsibilities of family and started helping his brothers for livelihood by working at garment shop. His belief that the tragedy of life doesn’t lie in not reaching your goal, the tragedy lies in having no goals to reach...
