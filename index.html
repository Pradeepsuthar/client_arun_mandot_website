About

Dream big and give back to the society as much as possible.

Hello, I'am Arun Mandot

A clairvoyant’s business tycoon born on November 21 st , comes from a humble background, Son of Late Shri Rang Lal Mandot and Smt. Tipu Bai Mandot, nurtures a sense of pride in roots that lie in the great tradition and glorious history of soil of Mewar, Having lost his father at a teenage of 7, the burden of nurturing the family came on the shoulder of elder brothers. At the age of 11, Arun shared the responsibilities of family and started helping his brothers for livelihood by working at garment shop. His belief that the tragedy of life doesn’t lie in not reaching your goal, the tragedy lies in having no goals to reach.

A thinker and a perfectionist, who can radiate you and linger on to awaken the best in you, with the belief that education should be based on the principle of learning through doing, from his teenage to now, from a kid to self-reliant, confident determined individual always had a desire to foray in education for others. Having stared the journey from SUN Institute of Management Studies from rented premises of 4000 sqft. To today wholly owned 5 Lac+ Sq.ft. area for various educational institutions under the umbrella of SUN Foundation and SIMS Sansthan.

More than 4500 graduates in Commerce, Management, Entrepreneurship and more than 1 Lac skill assessment under Skill Development Mission across the Nation are the witness of visionary intent though various organizations. Also credited to one of the finest school of Udaipur in holistic education that is Mount Litera Zee School, Udaipur. Presently a known name in entire region and associated on various Social, Educational, Religious, Corporate platforms and fulfilling share of duty through those.

Has gained a prominent position in society due to exemplary work done in past years. An inspiration for many with a genuine desire to give back to the society. Act as a magnet that attracts people and make them a part of life. A lesson to learn from this, that despite the odds, you can build massive success.
